Our Logo

The SpotX logo is the most fundamental element of our identity. It establishes brand ownership and creates visual consistency throughout our brand experience. The logo must always appear in one of the authorized formats within the authorized color palette. Always use the authorized artwork files, which can be scaled proportionally as needed. Use the vector versions where possible to keep the logo looking crisp and sharp. Do not redraw, modify, stretch or change the logo in any way. Do not typeset the logo with existing typefaces or use the logo in a sentence.
